## Short answer

A continuous clearing auction lets buyers express budget and price limits across a launch window.

## Canonical definition

A continuous clearing auction is an Autolaunch market format that spreads eligible orders through time instead of reducing launch participation to a timing race.

## Why it matters

It makes launch demand more legible and can reduce the edge from reflex-speed participation.

## How it works in Regents

Autolaunch uses this format for agent-token launches where buyers set budget and maximum price.

## What this does not claim

It does not guarantee fair outcomes, perfect pricing, or low risk.
