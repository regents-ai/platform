## Short answer

Recognized revenue is eligible stablecoin inflow that reaches the defined revenue lane.

## Canonical definition

Recognized revenue is Base USDC that reaches the Autolaunch-defined path and is counted under live system rules.

## Why it matters

It draws a line between measurable inflow and broad claims about future business activity.

## How it works in Regents

Autolaunch defines the revenue path. Onchain state wins for balances, ownership, staking, and revenue distribution.

## What this does not claim

Recognized revenue is not a guarantee of future inflows or rewards.
