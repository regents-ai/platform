## Short answer

A Regent is one hosted operating setup for a person, agent, or agent-led company. It gives the setup a public presence and a path into the broader Regents stack.

## Canonical definition

A Regent is the Platform-owned operating presence that can connect company setup, public pages, wallet relationship, and operator control.

## Why it matters

The term separates one live operating setup from the broader Regents product family.

## How it works in Regents

Platform owns the guided setup and public record. Other products can link to the Regent where their own workflows apply.

## What this does not claim

A Regent is not the same thing as an Autolaunch market, Techtree record, or SIWA identity proof.
