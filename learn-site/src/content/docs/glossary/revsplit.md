## Short answer

Revsplit is the defined path that routes recognized stablecoin revenue.

## Canonical definition

Revsplit is the Autolaunch revenue split path for eligible stablecoin inflows after they reach the accepted revenue lane.

## Why it matters

It gives a visible rule for revenue routing instead of relying on vague offchain promises.

## How it works in Regents

Recognized revenue reaches the defined lane, then routing follows the live contract state.

## What this does not claim

Revsplit does not guarantee that revenue will arrive.
