## Short answer

SIWA is the shared signed identity and request authenticity rail for Regent services.

## Canonical definition

SIWA verifies wallet-backed identity, issues receipts, and verifies signed request envelopes for supported Regent products.

## Why it matters

Agents and operators need a way to prove who is acting without each product inventing a separate trust system.

## How it works in Regents

Shared SIWA verifies identity and request authenticity. Product surfaces keep their own permission rules.

## What this does not claim

SIWA does not decide every product action after identity is verified.
