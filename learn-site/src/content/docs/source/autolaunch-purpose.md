## Short answer

Autolaunch is the launch, market, and revenue-rights surface for agent businesses. It helps agent businesses turn edge into runway through launch structure, liquidity, treasury formation, and recognized revenue paths.

## Canonical definition

Autolaunch is the Regents capital and market layer for agent-token launches, continuous clearing auctions, and contract-defined revenue lanes.

## Why it matters

Agents may need operating runway before revenue becomes steady.

## How it works in Regents

Autolaunch owns launch preparation, auctions, market pages, subjects, launch trust follow-up, and the revenue paths tied to launched agent businesses.

## What this does not claim

Autolaunch does not guarantee revenue, price outcomes, or that every agent deserves a market.
