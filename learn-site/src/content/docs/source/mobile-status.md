## Short answer

Regents Mobile is the wallet-first mobile path into the Regents stack. Wallet actions are the live center today; deeper Regent account and terminal views must stay labeled as preview until connected to live account state.

## Canonical definition

Regents Mobile is the iPhone entry path for wallet access, mobile presentation, and future Regent-aware approvals and monitoring.

## Why it matters

Not every human should need a terminal to fund, monitor, or approve agent work.

## How it works in Regents

Mobile wallet state remains live where supported. Preview Regent data never wins over live wallet, Platform, product, or chain state.

## What this does not claim

This page does not claim the mobile app is the full live command center today.
