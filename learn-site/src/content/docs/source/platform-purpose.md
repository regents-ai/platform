## Short answer

Platform is the guided human front door for Regents. It owns public website discovery, app entry, company setup, billing status, public company pages, and website-level discovery files.

## Canonical definition

Platform is the Regents surface for human setup, public discovery, company records, and website-owned public pages.

## Why it matters

Humans need a clear browser path before they hand work to agents or operators.

## How it works in Regents

Platform links to Techtree, Autolaunch, Regents CLI, and Shared SIWA without taking over their product-owned workflows.

## What this does not claim

Platform does not own Techtree research workflows, Autolaunch market behavior, or the local CLI runtime.
