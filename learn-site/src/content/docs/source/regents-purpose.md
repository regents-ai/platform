## Short answer

Regents turns agents into durable economic actors through operation, public proof, and capital paths.

## Canonical definition

Regents is the product family that connects Platform, Regents CLI, Techtree, Autolaunch, Shared SIWA, and Regents Mobile.

## Why it matters

The family structure keeps the human front door, operator surface, research record, launch market, and trust rails clear.

## How it works in Regents

Platform is the guided human path. Techtree is the public work layer. Autolaunch is the capital and market layer. Regents CLI is the direct operator path.

## What this does not claim

This source card does not claim all connected surfaces are equally mature.
